About Connector Jewels...It’s the same only different!

Connector Jewels interchangeable, four-piece necklace design can be worn more than 12 different ways. We source materials from around the globe and have developed a core range of products. We also design and develop many one-of-a-kind or limited-edition pieces. With hundred of styles to choose from, you are sure to find one that fits your personal style.

Both our product and company were designed with three core principles in mind: Quality, Community, and Funnity.

Quality

Connector Jewels features high-quality, hand-crafted and hand-finished pieces featuring both precious and semi-precious stones and materials. Our unique Connector Clasp, which makes each style flexible and interchangeable, is designed with high-grade stainless steel that prevents tarnish and allergic reactions.

When you purchase Connector Jewel can also expect a high quality of customer service. Should you have any queries or problems with our product, contact us so we can keep your connection with us a enjoyable one.

Funnity

Connector Jewels isn’t just fun product, it's an experience! No matter the style— from classic pearls to unique charms— all our necklaces and pieces are designed to be shared and connected with family and friends.

Community

We are proud that Connector Jewels is a product for women, by women. We work directly with and purchases all of our pieces from artisans living in developing and third world countries. We are thrilled that the work of ethically creating Connect Jewels provides economic opportunities for the artisans and their families.
